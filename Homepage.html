Privacy Awareness

This webpage is designed to teach you all about privacy awareness online, both when surfing the web and also when using apps on your phone.
Click on the navigation bar on top of the page to learn more about the different subjects.

Why is it important to know about privacy rules?

There are many reasons why it is important to know how privacy rules and regulations work.
The most important one is so you are aware of the information you leave when you visit a website or download an app.
